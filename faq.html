Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the implementation of a Chatbot take?

A chatbot deployment can take anything from a few days to a few weeks, depending on the amount of time the individual or team in charge of your chatbot has available in addition to their other responsibilities. No technical knowledge is necessary to have a chatbot prototype up and operating in a matter of hours.

When you deal with a solution provider who knows what they're doing, installing a chatbot requires very little time and resources, and it can be completed in a matter of weeks. Advanced solution providers, on the other hand, effectively supply a platform with everything pre-installed and ready to use.

Configuring the conversations to match the specified use case takes up the majority of the labor during chatbot installation. IT and development resources are reduced to a bare minimum because little or no coding is required. Simple, basic tools allow users to quickly conceive, construct, and test conversations. Without any technological skills, teams can easily design and deploy a chatbot that is ready to use.
