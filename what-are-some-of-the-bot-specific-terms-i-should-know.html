Frequently Asked Questions

What are some of the bot-specific terms I should know?

Chatbot: A software program that imitates human-like user interaction.

builder: A platform for creating and launching chatbots.

channel: A location where a chatbot can be deployed and with which people can engage.

intelligence: A subfield of computer science that is frequently referred to as AI.

language processing(NLP): A subfield of artificial intelligence that aids computers in reading, comprehending, and making sense of human language.

Autoresponder: When a user delivers their first message, or a certain phrase, to the bot, an automated message is sent.

Broadcast: A message that is sent to all of your chatbot users.

Compulsory input: A piece of information users must enter before moving on to the next level.

Conversational UI: Everything that surrounds words is referred to as conversational UI.

Conversational UX: The experience of conversing with the bot is referred to as conversational UX.
